How to covert an image to 832 pixel wide grayscale one:

convert IMG_7245.JPG -resize 832 -set colorspace RGB -colors 16 \
  +dither -colorspace gray ~/franek.png

Thermal-printer mechanism.

  • Soldered wires
  • Thermal heads are connected using 1.25 mm pitch FFC connectors.
